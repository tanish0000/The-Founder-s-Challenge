Welcome to The founders Challange

What is The Founders challange

Hyderabad's one of of the biggest- Pitching challange for student Entrepreneurs.

Having a business idea but waiting for opportunities like Shark Tank? Well, The Founders Challenge is here. Come pitch your ideas to a panel of CEOs and VCs, impress them, and get your initial funding and mentorship. Even if you don't have an idea, you are still welcome. You can connect with like-minded people across various domains and find your next co-founder, investor, or tech team.
